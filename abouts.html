Cybersccurity academY – India’s Largest Skill Development Platform

Cybersccurity academy started physical operations on November 5th, 2016, we owned and operated physical institutions in the state of Kerala, due to the Corona virus Pandemic we moved our operations online and began teaching students through Avodha.com. The platform quickly took off and we began rapidly expanding to Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Telugu speaking states as well as the Hindi Heartland. Currently, our moto is to teach students in the vernacular and to ensure that we bring about a revolution which will promote the Learn Today, Pay the Fees After you Land a Job model that we have pioneered in India.
